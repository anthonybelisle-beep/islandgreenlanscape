FAQ

Common Questions

Do you provide free estimates?
Yes — all estimates are completely free and come with no obligation. We'll visit your property, discuss your goals, and provide a clear, detailed quote.
How quickly can you start a project?
Start times vary depending on the season and our current schedule. We'll give you a realistic timeline during your estimate. Maintenance and smaller projects often have faster turnaround than major installations.
Are you insured?
Yes. Island Green Services is fully insured and all team members are trained in safety procedures. You can hire us with complete confidence.
Do you offer ongoing maintenance contracts?
Absolutely. We offer recurring maintenance programs for both residential homeowners and strata/commercial properties — regular lawn care, seasonal clean-ups, pruning, and more.
What warranty comes with irrigation installation?
All irrigation systems installed by Island Green Services come with a 2-year warranty on parts and labour. We stand behind our work.
