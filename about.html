Who We Are

One Team, Every Outdoor Need

Island Green Services was founded with a simple premise: property owners shouldn't have to juggle multiple contractors to get their outdoor space looking its best.

We serve residential homeowners, strata corporations, and commercial clients across Campbell River and the Comox Valley. Whether it's a weekend refresh or a full property transformation, we bring the same attention to detail and commitment to quality.

Our History

Nearly Two Decades of Growing Strong

What started as a property maintenance operation has grown into one of Vancouver Island's most comprehensive outdoor service companies.

2006

Founded in Campbell River

Fadi Tawilah launches Island Green Services, focused on residential property maintenance and lawn care.

2009–2012

Expanding into Landscaping

Growing demand leads to full landscaping design and installation services — planting, sod, and garden development.

2013–2016

Irrigation & Excavating Added

The team expands with irrigation specialists and excavation equipment to handle complete outdoor projects from the ground up.

2017–Present

Full-Service Operation

With fencing, snow removal, strata maintenance, and commercial contracts added — Island Green becomes Vancouver Island's one-stop outdoor services provider.
